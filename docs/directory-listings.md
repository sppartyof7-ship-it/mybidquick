### Tagline / Short Description (under 150 chars)
Instant online quoting for cleaning companies. White-label quote pages that generate leads on autopilot.

### Full Description (250-400 words)

MyBidQuick is a white-label instant quoting platform built for cleaning and exterior service companies. It gives every business a branded quote page at their own subdomain (yourcompany.mybidquick.com) where customers can get instant cleaning quotes in under 60 seconds — no phone calls, no waiting.

When a customer visits the quote page, they enter their property details and select from tiered service packages (Standard, Premium, Platinum). MyBidQuick's smart upsell cascade automatically suggests related services — a house wash customer sees window cleaning, then gutter cleaning — increasing average ticket value by 40-60% without any manual sales effort.

Every quote submission delivers a qualified lead with the customer's name, email, phone, address, and exactly what they need. Leads flow into the built-in CRM dashboard with drag-and-drop Kanban boards, automated follow-up sequences, and conversion tracking.

Unlike traditional quoting software with monthly subscriptions, MyBidQuick uses a pay-per-lead credit model. Companies buy credit packs starting at $2.50/lead with no monthly fees and no contracts. New accounts get 3 free credits to start.

Key features include:
- White-label branding (logo, colors, custom subdomain)
- Smart upsell cascade (House Wash → Windows → Gutters)
- Three-tier pricing packages per service
- Built-in CRM with Kanban pipeline
- Auto follow-up email sequences
- QR code generator for marketing materials
- Embeddable quote widget (3 embed options)
- Mobile-optimized quote flow

MyBidQuick is ideal for pressure washing companies, house washing services, window cleaning businesses, gutter cleaning companies, and multi-service exterior cleaning operations.
